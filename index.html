About

I practice medicine in the Philippines — trained in ophthalmology at Philippine General Hospital, where I managed surgical and outpatient cases across the full breadth of ophthalmic disease: glaucoma, cornea, retina, pediatric ophthalmology, and oculoplastics.

Before medicine, I studied geology at UP Diliman: how the earth stratifies, how time leaves its record in rock. Both disciplines ask the same question with different instruments — what happened here, and why?

More recently, I have been building digital tools for the clinic — an AI-assisted triage system for ophthalmology referrals, a platform for generating medical certificates, and an online intake tool for patient histories. The work sits at the edge of medicine and software, which is where I prefer to be.
