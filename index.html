PART 1

About

I build AI-powered products at the intersection of data and real-world decisions. My current focus is GroundCheck — a platform that helps land buyers assess whether a property is the right fit, using AI to surface what matters before money changes hands. Alongside that, I've built a suite of AI-powered medical assistive tools designed to extend what clinicians can do in under-resourced settings.

I studied geology at UP Diliman — how the earth stratifies, how patterns in physical data tell a story about what's underneath. That lens travels: what does the data say, and what decision does it unlock?

I'm a licensed physician with clinical experience across primary care and tertiary hospital settings. That background shapes how I build in healthcare — with a bias for tools that are actually usable at the point of care.
