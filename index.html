About

I practice medicine in the Philippines. I served as a physician in the Ophthalmology department of Philippine General Hospital, managing surgical and outpatient cases across the full breadth of ophthalmic disease: glaucoma, cornea, retina, pediatric eye disease, and oculoplastics.

Before medicine, I studied geology at UP Diliman: how the earth stratifies, how time leaves its record in rock. Both disciplines ask the same question with different instruments: what happened here, and why?

More recently I've been building digital tools for the clinic, work that sits at the edge of medicine and software, which is where I prefer to be.
